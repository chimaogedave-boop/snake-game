Snake Game

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move. Eat food, grow longer, and avoid the walls and your own tail.

On phones, use the touch controls under the board.

How It Works

  • The board is a 20 by 20 grid drawn on a canvas.
  • The snake is stored as a list of grid cells.
  • Each game tick adds a new head and removes the tail.
  • If the snake eats food, we skip removing the tail once.
